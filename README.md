# EchoStream

**Turn one long-form video into 20+ viral short clips — powered by AI.**

EchoStream is a fictional video processing SaaS landing page prototype. It explores what a modern, creator-focused AI platform could look like.

## Concept

EchoStream is designed for content creators, agencies, and marketers who want to repurpose long videos into platform-ready short clips for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts — all without manual editing.

The concept covers a full SaaS landing page including a hero section, features, pricing, testimonials, and FAQ.

## Design & Branding

**Color Palette**

The palette draws from the warmth of creator platforms — think YouTube red and Instagram orange. The primary accent is a bold red-orange (`#FF4D1C`), paired with a softer warm orange (`#FF7A3C`) for gradients and highlights. Backgrounds are deep near-blacks (`#0D0D0D`, `#111111`) for the dark mode default, with clean whites and warm off-whites for the light mode variant. Subtle glow effects using the accent color add depth to cards and interactive elements.

**Typography**

Two typefaces work together throughout the design. **Sora** handles the logo and display-level headings — its geometric character gives EchoStream a techy, modern feel. **Plus Jakarta Sans** covers all body copy, UI labels, and navigation — it's clean, readable, and slightly humanist, which keeps the interface from feeling cold. Together they balance personality with clarity.

**Style**

The overall aesthetic is dark-first, minimal, and creator-friendly. Cards use soft borders and subtle backgrounds rather than heavy shadows. Accent tags and labels are pill-shaped with a warm glow. Scroll-triggered reveal animations bring sections in gently. The nav blurs and darkens on scroll using a frosted glass effect. Both dark and light modes are fully supported with a toggle.
